## Pricing Rationale

### Cost Structure

**Per-User Monthly Costs:**
```
AI API (OpenRouter/Claude):  $3-15/user (depending on usage)
Database (Neon):             $0.50/user
Storage (R2):                $0.25/user (50GB average)
Infrastructure:              $1/user
Support:                     $2/user (amortized)
---
Total Cost:                  $6.75 - $18.75/user
```

**Margins:**
- **Purr Plan:** 52% margin ($9 - $4.32 cost)
- **Meow Plan:** 66% margin ($29 - $9.86 cost)
- **Roar Plan:** 81% margin ($99 - $18.81 cost)

### Competitive Analysis

| Competitor | Price | Messages | Our Advantage |
|------------|-------|----------|---------------|
| ChatGPT Plus | $20/mo | Unlimited | Personality, desktop sync |
| Claude Pro | $20/mo | Unlimited | Desktop sync, cat theme |
| Perplexity Pro | $20/mo | 600/day | MeowConnect, multi-model |
| Poe | $20/mo | Varies | Cheaper tiers, unique UX |

**Our Position:** Premium niche product with unique value (cat personality, desktop sync, social features)

## Pricing Psychology

### Why These Prices Work:

1. **$9 (Purr):** Under $10 psychological barrier, impulse purchase
2. **$29 (Meow):** Premium sweet spot, 3x value perception
3. **$99 (Roar):** Enterprise signal, team budgets

### Anchoring Effect:
- Show $99 Roar plan first
- Makes $29 Meow look like great value
- Makes $9 Purr look like a steal

### Annual Discount:
- 17% savings encourages long-term commitment
- Improves cash flow
- Reduces churn

## Churn Prevention

### At-Risk User Alerts
When user approaches message limit:

### Win-Back Campaign
For churned subscribers:
- Wait 30 days
